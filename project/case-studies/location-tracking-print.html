The Problem

Why Fieldproxy Was Losing Enterprise Deals

In Q2 2023, Fieldproxy had a product problem that wasn't obvious from the outside. Our AI dispatch engine was best-in-class. Our mobile app was loved by technicians. But when we tried to win enterprise accounts (100+ technician operations), we kept losing deals to Skedulo and Verizon Connect.

The Real Reason: Enterprise managers couldn't see what their technicians were actually doing in the field in real-time.

Without live location tracking, Fieldproxy felt like a scheduling tool, not an operations command center. Large companies needed to trust their field operations. We couldn't provide that visibility.

The Stakes: Our CROs estimated we were losing ~$4M in annual enterprise pipeline because location tracking was a table-stakes requirement in RFPs. It wasn't a nice-to-have feature—it was a prerequisite to the conversation. Without it, we couldn't compete for $500K+ ARR accounts.

What Enterprise Customers Actually Needed

Real-Time Visibility

"Where are my technicians right now?" Not historical. Not delayed. Live. They needed to make decisions in the moment.

Trust Verification

"Did John actually go to that job site?" Location + timestamp as proof. Removes ambiguity in disputes and billing.

Operational Decisions

"Can we reassign this job to someone closer?" Real-time location data enables dynamic dispatch—a competitive advantage.

Compliance & Payroll

Track hours and locations for labor audits and billing accuracy. Location data is required for enterprise compliance.
